In center of window there is an 'Earth' that applies a gravitational force on any object in the system. When the user right clicks on the window an 'asteroid' will spawn under the cursor. The asteroid will have a random mass, direction and velocity. 

The objects will be attracted by the Earths gravity, some asteroids may be lost to space but some will be caught in an eliptical orbit around the earth.

Right click - Spawn asteroid. 
Right Key - Increase speed.
Left Key - Decrease Speed.
Down - Display/Remove speed. 
Up - Play/pause.
Backspace - Backgrund Refresh.
